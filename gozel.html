Dr Gözel Shakeri

I am a post-doctoral research associate at the School of Computing Science, University of Glasgow. I work on the RadioMe project. I defended my Ph.D. thesis successfully in spring 2020. Please find my CV here, and find the link to my GitHub repository here.

My research interests focus on Sustainable Human-Computer Interaction (HCI), using behaviour change intervention science to create interfaces that support online shoppers in purchasing eco-friendly products. My work has a strong experimental focus, applying multidisciplinary evidence to create rich, natural, and sustainable interaction between human and technology. I am one of Europe’s few Sustainable HCI researchers.

I have 15 publications (first author on twelve). Citations of my research show great depth and strong relevance to the field of HCI. I target my work at top venues in my field. The GIST group that I am a member of has the largest number of papers of any University in the world at the ACM MobileHCI conference, the top venue for mobile research.
